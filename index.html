"F" design pattern

The "F" design pattern is a layout design for a website based on where a user is usually determined to look. The way the user views or reads a website is in the shape of an "F", with the user looking at the top left of the screen then looking horizontally across the screen. After that the user will look at the second horizontal bar, which is the lower bar of an "F". Finally will read the website vertically.


Logo


Now we do not have to make a website that is in the shape of an "F", but the website should be structured in a way so that people who are reading in an "F" shape be accounted for.

The reason why it is important to base your website around the "F" design pattern is because if you have valuable information in an area where the "F" reading area is not accounted for, then many users may miss that valuable information.

A majority of website will have their logo at the top left of the screen, this is because the logo fits in the range of the "F" shaped viewing area. This is crucial to any website.
